Some of my favorite elements of yesteryear

As the web evolves, so do tastes in webpage design and as a result, many elements get left behind in most webpages. Here are some of what we lost in our pursuit of progress.

  • An abundance of GIFs
  • abusing iframes to make permanent headerbars
  • intro movies made in Adobe Flash
  • the <blink> tag
  • making literal maps you can click on
