The #1 Song of the week of June 27, 2001

Lady Marmalade - Christina Aguilera, Lil' Kim, Mýa and Pink

On the week of the 27th of June 2001, the number one song was the 2001 cover of Lady Marmalade by Christina Aguilera, Lil' Kim, Mýa and Pink. Originally released in 1974 by the soul band Labelle, this version was made for the 2001 movie Moulin Rouge! and its soundtrack.

This song was on the #1 chart for 5 weeks after the 8th week it was on the Billboard top 100. It's also the ninth ever song to top Billboard twice when performed by different artists. It used to hold the record for longest number 1 single by a female rapper, until 2014 when Iggy Azalea's Fancy topped the chart for 7 consecutive weeks.
