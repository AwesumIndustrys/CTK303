Home

You press the button to go to earth. Instantly, the ship rockets itself in that direction. You can feel the G-forces pinning you to the wall.

Unfortunately, the ship didn't account for earth's tendency to move so it crash lands into a random coastline, flinging you out of it and into the sand. This hurts like hell, but you still make it out alive. You did it. You're back on your home planet, even if you're not sure in what country, you're still a step closer in returning to your home.
