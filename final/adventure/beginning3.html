Steel beam land

You go through it and find yourself in some sort of industrial area. Steel girders, ladders, and whatnot make up the contents of this room. It's also fairly well lit with what looks like flourescent lighting. If you turn around, you see the fake fields you came out of and the outside reveals it was made out of canvas, wood, and a massive incandescent light bulb for the fake sunlight. But now what? There doesn't seem to be anyone around and this place is so big you don't really have a clue on where to go.
