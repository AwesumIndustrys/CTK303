Still empty

You get in front of a door and decide to open it. It's somehow unlocked. You keep wandering but still see no signs of any other life. You look out a window and see a vast empty space. There are a few stars and space rocks, but no planets. What happened? Where are you? Where is everyone else? How did you get here. What's going on?

These questions and many more like it run through your head, freaking yourself out more and more until you pass out. Time passes and you eventually wake up, in the same place as before. There are a couple of other doors you can find, but you're starting to feel rather hungry. Hopefully this ship isn't so abandoned that you can't find anything remotely edible.
