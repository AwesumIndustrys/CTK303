Kitchen

You walk through the door into what looks to be a kitchen-type room. You rummage through the fridge and the cupboards, hoping to find something. After looking through so many places, and considering how much nutritional value particle board has, you finally find some sort of preserved rations. You tear open the bag and inspect the food-type thing. It looks like it's supposed to be ground meat and some sort of noodle. Thankfully, no visible mold has grown on it so you decide to give it a shot. It tastes terrible but it at least won't make you sick, probably. You finish it off and press on.
